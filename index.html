The Seneca Woman

Smart. Sophisticated. Social.

The Seneca woman is intellectually curious, confident in her presence, and deeply invested in friendship and community. These qualities shape both the culture of the organization and the experience of membership.

Smart

Curious, ambitious, and engaged with the ideas and opportunities that define Harvard life.

Sophisticated

Thoughtful, poised, and intentional in the way she leads, contributes, and carries herself.

Social

Warm, connected, and committed to building the kind of community others want to be part of.

A commitment beyond the Seneca

The Via Faciam Foundation

Some efforts extend beyond our own community. Through the Via Faciam Foundation, members and alumnae support initiatives dedicated to opportunity, leadership, and meaningful impact.

Future Vision

A permanent home for women’s community at Harvard

The Seneca is working toward establishing a permanent home near campus: a space for connection, collaboration, philanthropy, and the long-term growth of women’s community at Harvard.

This vision reflects an investment not only in The Seneca itself, but in future generations of women across the university community.
