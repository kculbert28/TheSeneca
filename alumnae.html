Alumnae

A network for life

The Seneca experience extends far beyond graduation. Alumnae remain an active and essential part of the community through mentorship, friendship, philanthropy, and professional support.

Lifelong connection across generations

Membership in The Seneca is designed to endure long after college. Alumnae continue to shape the organization through mentorship, leadership, philanthropy, and lasting personal relationships that connect generations of Seneca women.

From Cambridge to cities around the world, alumnae remain united by shared experiences, enduring friendships, and a continued investment in the future of the organization.

Ways alumnae remain involved

Mentorship & Guidance

Alumnae provide mentorship and career guidance for undergraduate members across a wide range of industries, experiences, and professional paths.

Events & Gatherings

Reunions, dinners, networking events, and seasonal gatherings help sustain friendships and strengthen connections over time.

Professional Network

The Seneca alumnae network creates opportunities for collaboration, encouragement, and support across generations and geographic locations.

Community & Friendship

Beyond professional connection, alumnae remain deeply involved in the personal relationships and traditions that define The Seneca experience.

Giving Back

Supporting future generations of Seneca women

Alumnae play a central role in shaping the future of The Seneca. Through leadership, mentorship, and philanthropy, alumnae help sustain the organization’s mission while ensuring future generations of women have access to the same opportunities, friendships, and support systems that have defined The Seneca experience for decades.

Their continued involvement strengthens both the organization itself and its broader impact across the Harvard community.

A Global Community

Relationships that continue to grow over time

The Seneca network extends far beyond campus. Alumnae remain connected through friendships, professional relationships, mentorship, and shared traditions that continue across cities, industries, and stages of life.

Whether gathering during reunion weekends, attending summer events, or reconnecting through mentorship and philanthropy, Seneca women continue building relationships that remain meaningful for years to come.

Once a Seneca woman, always a Seneca woman.
